How do we calculate global markets?

  1. Add up quantity demanded.
  2. Add up quantity supplied.
  3. Find the price at which Quantity Demanded = Quantity Supplied ($5, 500 lbs of coffee).
  4. Since Guatamala produces 360lbs and only demands 280lbs, it exports 360-280 = 80lbs to Croatia.
